Groups

Groups let you combine accounts by tag combinations and view them in the Overview. Each group appears as a stat card and a chart line.

Import historical data

Paste your existing monthly tracking sheet (wide format: account names down the first column, months across the top). Copy/paste from Google Sheets works (tab-separated). The app will auto-detect months and let you map source rows to your accounts before importing.
